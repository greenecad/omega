The Omega Games is a competition open to all students in Conroe High School.

  • Complete challenges to earn points
  • Surpass your peers
  • Prove that you alone are the Omega Games champion.

The Omega Games are broken into two rounds.

Round 1

  • Virtual competition
  • Open to anyone
  • Complete challenges that open at regular intervals to earn points.

Round 2

  • In-person competition
  • Top 12 competitors from round 1 advance to round two
  • Various elimination rounds are held until only one person remains.

The grand prize? $100

FAQ:

Who all can participate?

  • Any student currently enrolled at Conroe High School can play.
  • However, Freshmen students may find certain challenges very difficult unless they have classes at the main campus.

What types of challenges are there?

  • Challenges will be released at regular intervals throughout the competition.
  • Most challenges will require either submission of a code or photo proof of an action.
  • Other challenges may involve completion of an embedded task, or participation in a live event.
  • Photo submission challenges will require manual verification before the points are awarded.
  • Challenges will award different amounts of points based on the difficulty of the task.
